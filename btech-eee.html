⚡ EEE Career Roadmaps

Find specialized paths for Electrical & Electronics Engineering. Track power, machines, and control grids.

🕸️

Power Systems

Master transmission lines, load flows (Newton-Raphson), symmetrical/unsymmetrical faults, and protection relay operations.

16 WEEKS • 4 PHASES →
🎛️

Power Electronics

Learn about power devices (SCR, IGBT), converters, buck/boost choppers, inverters, and PWM switching systems.

16 WEEKS • 4 PHASES →
📈

Control Systems

Master transfer functions, block reduction, time response, and stability analysis (Root Locus, Bode, Nyquist plots).

16 WEEKS • 4 PHASES →
🌀

Electrical Machines

Study DC motors/generators starting and speed control, single/three-phase transformers, alternators, and induction motors.

16 WEEKS • 4 PHASES →

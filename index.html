🎓 B.Tech Roadmaps

Software Engineering

Web Development

Master frontend design and robust backend APIs. Learn HTML, CSS, modern JavaScript, database modeling, and Express servers.

16 WEEKS • 4 PHASES →

Data Analytics

Extract meaning from raw data. Master Excel spreadsheets, query databases with SQL, manipulate data with Pandas, build dashboards in Tableau/Power BI, and apply ML forecasting.

16 WEEKS • 4 PHASES →

Data Science

Extract insights from raw datasets. Build a foundation in statistics, data cleaning, exploratory visual analysis, and classical ML models.

16 WEEKS • 4 PHASES →

Machine Learning / AI

Dive deep into core mathematical concepts, neural network architectures, PyTorch, Large Language Models (LLMs), and agent workflows.

20 WEEKS • 4 PHASES →

DevOps & Cloud

Build automated, scalable infrastructures. Master Linux systems, Docker containerization, Kubernetes orchestration, CI/CD, and AWS services.

16 WEEKS • 4 PHASES →

Mobile Development

Construct responsive applications for mobile screens. Study Kotlin for Android, Swift for iOS, reactive layouts, APIs, and publishing.

16 WEEKS • 4 PHASES →

Cybersecurity

Defend computing environments. Explore network security, pen testing web apps, privilege escalation, threat hunting, and SIEM analysis.

16 WEEKS • 4 PHASES →

DSA & CP

Excel in coding interviews and technical evaluations. Master data structures, algorithmic paradigms (Greedy, DP), and patterns-based problems.

16 WEEKS • 4 PHASES →

Hardware Engineering

ECE (Electronics & Communication)

Study semiconductors, digital and analog designs, microcontrollers, embedded protocols, and signal processing DSP systems.

16 WEEKS • 4 PHASES →

EEE (Electrical & Electronics)

Master electric AC/DC machinery, transformers, power grids analysis, control feedback loops, and power electronics choppers/inverters.

16 WEEKS • 4 PHASES →

Mechanical Engineering

Study structural mechanics, stress failure, Rankine cycles, thermodynamics, fluid dynamics boundary layers, casting, and CAD modeling.

16 WEEKS • 4 PHASES →

🏛️ Government Exams

Central Government Exams

UPSC IAS Exam

Prepare systematically for Prelims (GS & CSAT), Mains GS I-IV papers, Essay writing, policy research, and final interview panels.

24 WEEKS • 4 PHASES →

SSC CGL Exam

Master Arithmetic, Advanced Algebra, Geometry, English grammar/vocabulary, Reasoning, and General Awareness for Tier 1 & 2 exams.

16 WEEKS • 4 PHASES →

GATE Exam

Master General Aptitude, Engineering Mathematics, and core technical concepts of your branch for M.Tech admissions and PSU jobs.

20 WEEKS • 4 PHASES →

IBPS Banking PO/Clerk

Excel in speed arithmetic, complex seating puzzles, English comprehension, RBI monetary policies, and banking awareness.

16 WEEKS • 4 PHASES →

State Government Exams

SI Police Exam

Prepare for state SI written evaluations covering General Studies, Arithmetic, Reasoning, Language tests, and physical guidelines.

16 WEEKS • 4 PHASES →

Group 1 Exam

Prepare for top administrative services (Deputy Collector/DSP) covering Prelims GS and comprehensive analytical Mains writing.

20 WEEKS • 4 PHASES →

Group 2 & 3 Exams

Track syllabus for sub-registrar, municipal commissioners, and executive officer posts. Master Polity, Economy, and Math.

16 WEEKS • 4 PHASES →

State PSC Exam

Prepare for state civil service positions focusing on state geography, culture, historical dynasties, budgets, and district administration.

20 WEEKS • 4 PHASES →

Resume Keywords

Core Interview Focus

  • Deriving the Deal-Grove model equations for thermal oxidation.
  • Contrasting positive and negative photoresists in terms of chemistry.
  • Describing the differences between wet etching and reactive ion etching.
  • Explaining the ion implantation doping process and annealing.
  • Detailing the dual-damascene process for copper interconnects.

Standard Preparation

  • Review textbooks and past exam question databases.
  • Practice timed numerical solving and multiple-choice questions.
  • Develop comprehensive subject-wise summary sheets.
  • Participate in mock tests to analyze error diagnostics.

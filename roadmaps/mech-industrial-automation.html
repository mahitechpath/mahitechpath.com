Placement & Interview Strategy

Written tests, concept evaluations, and practical design challenges overview.

Resume Keywords

  • PLC Programming (Ladder)
  • SCADA / HMI Design
  • Pneumatic Circuit Designs
  • Industrial Sensors Integration
  • Structured Text (ST) Code
  • VFD Motor Controllers

Core Interview Focus

  • Wiring sink/source sensors to a PLC input module diagram.
  • Explaining the difference between TON and TOF timers in ladder logic.
  • Designing a pneumatic circuit to sequence two cylinders (A+ B+ A- B-).
  • Comparing Modbus RTU and Modbus TCP protocols.
  • Detailing the SIL categories in industrial safety.

Standard Preparation

  • Review textbooks and past exam question databases.
  • Practice timed numerical solving and multiple-choice questions.
  • Develop comprehensive subject-wise summary sheets.
  • Participate in mock tests to analyze error diagnostics.

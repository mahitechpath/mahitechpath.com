Placement & Interview Strategy

Written tests, concept evaluations, and practical design challenges overview.

Resume Keywords

  • Data Center Operations
  • Server Hardware Diagnostics

Core Interview Focus

  • Detailing the differences between RAID 5 and RAID 10 in terms of drives and parity.
  • Explaining the purpose of hot/cold aisle containment in data centers.
  • Contrasting single-mode and multi-mode fiber optics protocols.
  • Describing how out-of-band management (IPMI) accesses a server.
  • Detailing the steps taken during a hardware server RAM failure.

Standard Preparation

  • Review textbooks and past exam question databases.
  • Practice timed numerical solving and multiple-choice questions.
  • Develop comprehensive subject-wise summary sheets.
  • Participate in mock tests to analyze error diagnostics.

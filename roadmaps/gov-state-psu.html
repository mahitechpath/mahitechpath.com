Exam Strategy & Selection Guide

Overview of the stages of selection, syllabus details, and recommended resources.

Exam Pattern & Syllabus

  • State PSU Officer Entrance
  • Public Sector Financial Accounting
  • Government Procurement (GeM)
  • Operations & Inventory Management
  • Contract & SLA Formulation
  • Industrial & Labor Law Compliance

Core Selection Tips

  • Detailing the steps to execute a major procurement tender on GeM.
  • Explaining the role of CAG in auditing state public undertakings.
  • Resolving a simulated labor dispute in a state utility plant.
  • Discussing the financial health of power distribution companies (DISCOMs).
  • Demonstrating ethical parameters during vendor evaluations.

Reference Resources

  • Standard NCERT school textbooks (Class VI - XII)
  • Daily Newspapers: The Hindu or Indian Express
  • Official Government Portals (PIB, Yojana, Kurukshetra)
  • Mock test series papers and past year questions
  • Subject-specific standard reference books

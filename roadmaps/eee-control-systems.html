Placement & Interview Strategy

Written tests, concept evaluations, and practical design challenges overview.

Resume Keywords

  • Closed-Loop Control Systems
  • PID Controller Design & Tuning
  • Root Locus & Bode Plots
  • Nyquist Stability Criterion
  • State-Space & Pole Placement
  • Compensator Design (Lead/Lag)

Core Interview Focus

  • Deriving the steady-state error for step, ramp, and parabolic inputs for Type 0, 1, 2 systems.
  • Explaining the physical significance of gain margin and phase margin.
  • Designing a lead compensator to improve the transient response phase margin.
  • Describing the Jury's stability test and comparing it to Routh-Hurwitz in z-domain.
  • Detailing the effect of adding a zero to a second-order system's root locus.

Standard Preparation

  • Review textbooks and past exam question databases.
  • Practice timed numerical solving and multiple-choice questions.
  • Develop comprehensive subject-wise summary sheets.
  • Participate in mock tests to analyze error diagnostics.

Placement & Interview Strategy

Written tests, concept evaluations, and practical design challenges overview.

Resume Keywords

  • Electro-Mechanical Systems
  • Sensor Signal Conditioning
  • Motor Drive Electronics
  • PID Control Implementation
  • Finite State Machines (C)
  • AutoCAD Electrical Panel Designs

Core Interview Focus

  • Designing an operational amplifier circuit to scale a 0-100mV sensor to 0-5V.
  • Explaining the purpose of a flyback diode in inductive load driver circuits.
  • Writing C code to decode quadrature signals from an encoder.
  • Tuning PID loops to minimize response time while avoiding overshoot.
  • Describing the difference between open-loop and closed-loop systems.

Standard Preparation

  • Review textbooks and past exam question databases.
  • Practice timed numerical solving and multiple-choice questions.
  • Develop comprehensive subject-wise summary sheets.
  • Participate in mock tests to analyze error diagnostics.

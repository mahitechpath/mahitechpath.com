📚 Exam Strategy & Preparation Guide

📋 Exam Pattern & Syllabus Overview

State PSC Assistant Engineer Exam. Paper I (General Studies MCQ), Paper II (Core Engineering Branch MCQ), and Personal/Technical Interview.

📖 Recommended Books List

  • • GATE Subject-wise Solved Question Banks
  • • PWD Standard Specifications Manual and Costing Codes
  • • State PSC AE Previous Solved Papers

🔑 Previous Year Paper Tips

Master PWD specific topics like billing (M-Book), BOQ estimation, and PERT/CPM project management, which frequently appear in both written exams and interviews.

🏆 Selection Process Steps

  • 1. State PSC AE Written Exam (Paper I GS & Paper II Technical)
  • 2. Technical & Personal Interview
  • 3. Document Verification of Engineering Credentials
  • 4. PWD / Irrigation department Assistant Engineer Allocation

🎯 Important Topics to Focus

Engineering Mathematics, Quantity Surveying & Cost Estimation, PWD Work Rules & Tenders, Core Technical Subjects (RCC Design/Network Theory/Fluid Mechanics).

Exam Strategy & Selection Guide

Overview of the stages of selection, syllabus details, and recommended resources.

Syllabus Details

  • State PSC Assistant Engineer Core
  • PWD Standard Specifications Manual
  • Quantity Surveying & Cost Estimation
  • Tender Document & Contract Rules
  • Project Management (CPM/PERT)
  • Geotechnical & Design Engineering

Core Selection Tips

  • Whiteboard derivation of shear force diagrams for a cantilever beam.
  • Explaining the process to take measurement readings in the PWD M-Book.
  • Detailing the concrete cube compression test procedure.
  • Resolving a simulated dispute regarding contractor extension requests.
  • Answering structural safety questions for rural irrigation canals.

Reference Resources

  • Standard NCERT school textbooks (Class VI - XII)
  • Daily Newspapers: The Hindu or Indian Express
  • Official Government Portals (PIB, Yojana, Kurukshetra)
  • Mock test series papers and past year questions
  • Subject-specific standard reference books

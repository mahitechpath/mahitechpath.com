Placement & Interview Strategy

Written tests, concept evaluations, and practical design challenges overview.

Resume Keywords

  • Penetration Testing (OSCP)
  • OWASP Top 10 Auditing
  • SIEM (Splunk / ELK)
  • Cryptography (AES/RSA)
  • Network Forensics (Wireshark)
  • Incident Response (NIST)

Core Interview Focus

  • Explaining the difference between symmetric and asymmetric encryption.
  • Describing how a SQL Injection works and detailing parameterized queries.
  • Explaining the 3-way handshake in TCP and how SYN floods exploit it.
  • Detailing the differences between Cross-Site Scripting (XSS) and CSRF.
  • Discussing privilege escalation vectors on Linux (SUID binaries).

Standard Preparation

  • Review textbooks and past exam question databases.
  • Practice timed numerical solving and multiple-choice questions.
  • Develop comprehensive subject-wise summary sheets.
  • Participate in mock tests to analyze error diagnostics.

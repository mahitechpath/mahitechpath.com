Exam Strategy & Selection Guide

Overview of the stages of selection, syllabus details, and recommended resources.

Exam Pattern & Syllabus

  • Criminal Justice & Law Enforcement
  • Internal Security & Intelligence
  • Indian Penal Code & CrPC
  • Modern Community Policing Models
  • Forensics & Crime Scene Management
  • Crisis & Disaster Management

Core Selection Tips

  • Justifying police actions in simulated law-and-order breakdowns.
  • Detailing structural modifications required to improve police-public relations.
  • Explaining legal checks against misuse of police authority.
  • Analyzing causes of cybercrime surges and proposing district WAF checks.
  • Demonstrating commitment to human rights in tough crime control scenarios.

Reference Resources

  • Standard NCERT school textbooks (Class VI - XII)
  • Daily Newspapers: The Hindu or Indian Express
  • Official Government Portals (PIB, Yojana, Kurukshetra)
  • Mock test series papers and past year questions
  • Subject-specific standard reference books

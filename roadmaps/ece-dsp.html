Placement & Interview Strategy

Written tests, concept evaluations, and practical design challenges overview.

Resume Keywords

  • Digital Signal Processing (DSP)
  • Filter Design (FIR/IIR)
  • FFT & Spectral Analysis
  • Multirate DSP & Filter Banks
  • Adaptive Filters (LMS/RLS)
  • MATLAB, Simulink & DSP C

Core Interview Focus

  • Explaining the Nyquist sampling theorem and detailing aliasing.
  • Deriving the bilinear transformation mapping from S-domain to Z-domain.
  • Comparing circular convolution and linear convolution, and explaining overlap-save.
  • Describing the butterfly structure of Radix-2 DIT-FFT and computing its complexity.
  • Explaining the finite word-length effects and limit cycles in digital filters.

Standard Preparation

  • Review textbooks and past exam question databases.
  • Practice timed numerical solving and multiple-choice questions.
  • Develop comprehensive subject-wise summary sheets.
  • Participate in mock tests to analyze error diagnostics.

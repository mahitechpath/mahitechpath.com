Placement & Interview Strategy

Written tests, concept evaluations, and practical design challenges overview.

Resume Keywords

  • VLSI Design & RTL Coding
  • Verilog HDL & SystemVerilog
  • Static Timing Analysis (STA)
  • ASIC/FPGA Design Flow
  • Physical Design (P&R) & CTS
  • DRC, LVS & Parasitic Extraction

Core Interview Focus

  • Deriving setup and hold time equations with clock skew and jitter.
  • Drawing and explaining CMOS inverter voltage transfer characteristics (VTC).
  • Explaining clock domain crossing (CDC) and 2-stage synchronizer design.
  • Describing scan chain insertion and why Design for Testability (DFT) is needed.
  • Contrasting blocking and non-blocking assignments in Verilog with hardware equivalents.

Standard Preparation

  • Review textbooks and past exam question databases.
  • Practice timed numerical solving and multiple-choice questions.
  • Develop comprehensive subject-wise summary sheets.
  • Participate in mock tests to analyze error diagnostics.

Placement & Interview Strategy

Written tests, concept evaluations, and practical design challenges overview.

Resume Keywords

  • Renewable Energy Design
  • Solar PV Plant Sizing
  • MPPT Control Algorithms
  • Grid Synchronization (PLL)
  • Microgrid Storage Systems
  • Simulation (Simulink / HOMER)

Core Interview Focus

  • Deriving the Betz limit for wind turbine power extraction.
  • Explaining the working and control loops of a DFIG in wind turbines.
  • Describing the Perturb & Observe MPPT algorithm steps.
  • Detailing anti-islanding protection and how it is implemented.
  • Calculating the Levelized Cost of Energy (LCOE) for a solar plant.

Standard Preparation

  • Review textbooks and past exam question databases.
  • Practice timed numerical solving and multiple-choice questions.
  • Develop comprehensive subject-wise summary sheets.
  • Participate in mock tests to analyze error diagnostics.

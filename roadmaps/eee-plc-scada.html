Placement & Interview Strategy

Written tests, concept evaluations, and practical design challenges overview.

Resume Keywords

  • PLC Programming (Ladder)
  • SCADA Dashboard Design
  • Industrial Networking (Modbus)
  • Analog I/O & Sensor Scaling
  • Structured Text & FBD
  • VFD Control & Starters

Core Interview Focus

  • Contrasting sink and source wiring configurations in PLC I/O.
  • Explaining the difference between TON and TOF timers.
  • Writing ladder logic for a star-delta motor starter with interlocking.
  • Describing Modbus registers mapping (Coils vs Holding Registers).
  • Detailing PID tuning parameters inside PLC configurations.

Standard Preparation

  • Review textbooks and past exam question databases.
  • Practice timed numerical solving and multiple-choice questions.
  • Develop comprehensive subject-wise summary sheets.
  • Participate in mock tests to analyze error diagnostics.

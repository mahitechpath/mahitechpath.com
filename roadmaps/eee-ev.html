Placement & Interview Strategy

Written tests, concept evaluations, and practical design challenges overview.

Resume Keywords

  • EV Powertrain Design
  • Traction Motor Control (FOC)
  • High-Voltage Safety (HVIL)
  • Battery Management Systems
  • MATLAB Simulink Modeling
  • Automotive CAN Telemetry

Core Interview Focus

  • Deriving the Clarke and Park coordinate transformations equations.
  • Explaining the transition from constant torque to constant power in traction motors.
  • Describing the operational sequence of contactors and pre-charge in high-voltage packs.
  • Detailing the differences between active and passive cell balancing.
  • Explaining the CC-CV charging profile of Lithium-ion batteries.

Standard Preparation

  • Review textbooks and past exam question databases.
  • Practice timed numerical solving and multiple-choice questions.
  • Develop comprehensive subject-wise summary sheets.
  • Participate in mock tests to analyze error diagnostics.

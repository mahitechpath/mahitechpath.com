Placement & Interview Strategy

Written tests, concept evaluations, and practical design challenges overview.

Resume Keywords

  • Wireless Communication (5G NR)
  • OFDM Systems Design
  • MIMO & Beamforming
  • SDR Prototyping (GNU Radio)
  • Channel Coding (LDPC/Polar)

Core Interview Focus

  • Explaining the purpose of the Cyclic Prefix in OFDM.
  • Describing spatial multiplexing and how SVD decomposes a MIMO channel.
  • Comparing 5G SA and NSA network architectures.
  • Explaining pilot contamination in massive MIMO systems.
  • Calculating the receiver sensitivity given noise figure and bandwidth.

Standard Preparation

  • Review textbooks and past exam question databases.
  • Practice timed numerical solving and multiple-choice questions.
  • Develop comprehensive subject-wise summary sheets.
  • Participate in mock tests to analyze error diagnostics.

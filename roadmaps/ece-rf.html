Placement & Interview Strategy

Written tests, concept evaluations, and practical design challenges overview.

Resume Keywords

  • RF Design Engineering
  • Impedance Matching (Smith Chart)
  • S-Parameter Analysis
  • Microstrip Filter Design (ADS)
  • LNA / Mixer Topologies
  • VNA / Spectrum Analyzer Testing

Core Interview Focus

  • Detailing S-parameters and what S11 return loss represents.
  • Designing a single-stub impedance matching network on a Smith Chart.
  • Deriving the noise figure of cascaded RF blocks (Friis formula).
  • Explaining the 1dB compression point and IP3 linearity metrics.
  • Describing microstrip routing guidelines to prevent crosstalk.

Standard Preparation

  • Review textbooks and past exam question databases.
  • Practice timed numerical solving and multiple-choice questions.
  • Develop comprehensive subject-wise summary sheets.
  • Participate in mock tests to analyze error diagnostics.

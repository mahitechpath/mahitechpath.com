Placement & Interview Strategy

Written tests, concept evaluations, and practical design challenges overview.

Resume Keywords

  • Mechanical Design & 3D CAD (SolidWorks)
  • Finite Element Analysis (FEA / ANSYS)
  • Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD)
  • Thermodynamics & Heat Transfer Analysis
  • Machine Design & Failure Analysis
  • Manufacturing Processes & CNC Machining

Core Interview Focus

  • Drawing shear force and bending moment diagrams for standard beam loadings.
  • Explaining the difference between the Maximum Shear Stress and Distortion Energy theories of failure.
  • Deriving the Taylor's tool life equation from cutting speed data.
  • Describing the physical significance of dimensionless numbers: Reynolds and Nusselt numbers.
  • Contrasting Goodman and Soderberg lines used in fatigue design limits.

Standard Preparation

  • Review textbooks and past exam question databases.
  • Practice timed numerical solving and multiple-choice questions.
  • Develop comprehensive subject-wise summary sheets.
  • Participate in mock tests to analyze error diagnostics.

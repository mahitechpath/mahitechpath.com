Placement & Interview Strategy

Written tests, concept evaluations, and practical design challenges overview.

Resume Keywords

  • Aerodynamic Analysis
  • Compressible Flow (Shock Waves)
  • Propulsion Cycles (Brayton)
  • Thin-Walled Shear Flow
  • Orbital Transfer Trajectories
  • CFD Simulation (ANSYS)

Core Interview Focus

  • Explaining the Prandtl lifting-line theory and induced drag.
  • Deriving the convergent-divergent nozzle area-Mach relation.
  • Calculating the shear center of an open channel thin-walled section.
  • Describing the dynamic modes of aircraft motion (Phugoid, Dutch Roll).
  • Detailing the Hohmann transfer steps and delta-V requirements.

Standard Preparation

  • Review textbooks and past exam question databases.
  • Practice timed numerical solving and multiple-choice questions.
  • Develop comprehensive subject-wise summary sheets.
  • Participate in mock tests to analyze error diagnostics.

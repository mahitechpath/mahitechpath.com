Core Interview Focus

  • Deriving the Merchant's shear angle relation in orthogonal metal cutting.
  • Calculating the riser dimensions using Caine's method for a given casting.
  • Explaining the V-I characteristics of an arc welder and calculating maximum power.
  • Describing the differences between hot and cold working of metals on grain structures.
  • Explaining Taylor's principle of gauge design for Go and No-Go gauges.

Standard Preparation

  • Review textbooks and past exam question databases.
  • Practice timed numerical solving and multiple-choice questions.
  • Develop comprehensive subject-wise summary sheets.
  • Participate in mock tests to analyze error diagnostics.

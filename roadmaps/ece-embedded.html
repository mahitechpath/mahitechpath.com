Placement & Interview Strategy

Written tests, concept evaluations, and practical design challenges overview.

Resume Keywords

  • Embedded C/C++ Programming
  • ARM Cortex-M & ESP32 Microcontrollers
  • Real-Time OS (FreeRTOS) Development
  • Protocols: I2C, SPI, UART, CAN Bus
  • Bare-metal Firmware & Driver Development
  • Debugging: JTAG, GDB, Logic Analyzers

Core Interview Focus

  • Explaining the volatile keyword and when it must be used.
  • Describing priority inversion and how to resolve it in RTOS.
  • Comparing SPI and I2C protocols in terms of speed, pins, and topologies.
  • Writing a thread-safe circular queue (ring buffer) implementation in C.
  • Explaining the interrupt handling process from hardware trigger to ISR execution.

Standard Preparation

  • Review textbooks and past exam question databases.
  • Practice timed numerical solving and multiple-choice questions.
  • Develop comprehensive subject-wise summary sheets.
  • Participate in mock tests to analyze error diagnostics.

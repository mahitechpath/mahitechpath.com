Core Interview Focus

  • Deriving the Betz limit for wind turbine power extraction.
  • Explaining the Hottel-Whillier-Bliss equation for flat plate collectors.
  • Designing a ground-loop heat exchanger for a geothermal heat pump.
  • Contrasting Pelton and Kaplan turbines based on head and flow rates.
  • Calculating the Levelized Cost of Energy (LCOE).

Standard Preparation

  • Develop comprehensive subject-wise summary sheets.

2. EV Battery Thermal Simulator

Build a thermal model in MATLAB to simulate temperature distributions in a 10s4p Lithium-ion battery pack under high discharge current.

Placement & Interview Strategy

Written tests, concept evaluations, and practical design challenges overview.

Resume Keywords

  • Automotive Drivetrain Design
  • IC Engine Performance & Tuning
  • Suspension & Steering Geometry
  • Brake Systems & ABS Dynamics
  • EV Powertrains & Battery Packs
  • CAD/CAE Vehicle Assembly

Core Interview Focus

  • Drawing and explaining the steering geometry for Ackerman steering.
  • Calculating the torque capacity of a single-plate clutch under uniform wear.
  • Explaining the working of a standard vehicle differential on corners.
  • Describing the operation of an Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) valve block.
  • Contrasting PMSM and Induction motors for high-performance EV traction.

Standard Preparation

  • Review textbooks and past exam question databases.
  • Practice timed numerical solving and multiple-choice questions.
  • Develop comprehensive subject-wise summary sheets.
  • Participate in mock tests to analyze error diagnostics.

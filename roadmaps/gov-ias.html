Exam Strategy & Selection Guide

Overview of the stages of selection, syllabus details, and recommended resources.

Exam Pattern & Syllabus

  • Civil Services Prelims GS (Paper I)
  • Mains General Studies GS I-IV
  • Indian Constitution & Polity
  • Public Administration & Governance
  • Ethics, Integrity & Case Studies
  • Policy Analysis & Research

Core Selection Tips

  • Presenting balanced administrative viewpoints on current national debates.
  • Explaining the constitutional validity of recent laws and judicial decisions.
  • Analyzing district-level development bottlenecks and proposing solutions.
  • Demonstrating ethical decision-making paths under simulated pressure.
  • Presenting home-state geography, administration, and cultural history.

Reference Resources

  • Standard NCERT school textbooks (Class VI - XII)
  • Daily Newspapers: The Hindu or Indian Express
  • Official Government Portals (PIB, Yojana, Kurukshetra)
  • Mock test series papers and past year questions
  • Subject-specific standard reference books

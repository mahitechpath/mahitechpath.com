Core Selection Tips

  • whiteboard derivation of a core branch formula (e.g. Rankine cycle).
  • Explaining the working principle of the target PSU's operations.
  • Analyzing the stability of a feedback control system using Bode plots.
  • Discussing the transition strategy of coal-fired PSUs to renewable energy.
  • Answering behavioral questions regarding work in remote industrial plants.

  • Official Government Portals (PIB, Yojana, Kurukshetra)
  • Mock test series papers and past year questions
  • Subject-specific standard reference books

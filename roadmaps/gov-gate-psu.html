📚 Exam Strategy & Preparation Guide

📋 Exam Pattern & Syllabus Overview

GATE Exam (65 questions, 100 marks MCQ/NAT/MSQ) followed by Group Discussions, Group Tasks, and Personal Interviews at individual PSUs.

📖 Recommended Books List

  • • Standard branch reference textbooks
  • • GATE Solved Past Year Papers (Made Easy or ACE Academy)
  • • Engineering Mathematics & General Aptitude Guides

🔑 Previous Year Paper Tips

Numerical Answer Type (NAT) questions carry high weightage and have no negative marking, but require absolute calculation accuracy. Solve full-length mocks regularly.

🏆 Selection Process Steps

  • 1. GATE National Level Online Entrance Examination
  • 2. Shortlisting by individual PSUs (BHEL, NTPC, ONGC, IOCL)
  • 3. Group Discussion (GD) & Group Task (GT) rounds
  • 4. Personal Interview (Technical & HR rounds) & Medical check

🎯 Important Topics to Focus

Engineering Mathematics & Calculus, General Aptitude & Reasoning, Branch Core Engineering Subjects (Thermodynamics/VLSI/Power Systems/Data Structures).

Exam Strategy & Selection Guide

Overview of the stages of selection, syllabus details, and recommended resources.

Syllabus Details

  • GATE Entrance Qualified
  • Advanced Engineering Mathematics
  • Core Branch Technical Expertise
  • General Aptitude & Data Analysis
  • PSU Group Discussion & HR Prep
  • Technical Project Management

Core Selection Tips

  • whiteboard derivation of a core branch formula (e.g. Rankine cycle).
  • Explaining the working principle of the target PSU's operations.
  • Analyzing the stability of a feedback control system using Bode plots.
  • Discussing the transition strategy of coal-fired PSUs to renewable energy.
  • Answering behavioral questions regarding work in remote industrial plants.

Reference Resources

  • Standard NCERT school textbooks (Class VI - XII)
  • Daily Newspapers: The Hindu or Indian Express
  • Official Government Portals (PIB, Yojana, Kurukshetra)
  • Mock test series papers and past year questions
  • Subject-specific standard reference books

Exam Strategy & Selection Guide

Overview of the stages of selection, syllabus details, and recommended resources.

Exam Pattern & Syllabus

  • CAPF Assistant Commandant Core
  • Border Management Surrounds
  • Internal Security & Counter-Insurgency
  • Argumentative Writing (Paper II)
  • Surveillance & Surrounding Tech
  • Disaster Management & Rescue

Core Selection Tips

  • Justifying troop deployments in simulated border incursions.
  • Contrasting the mandates of BSF, CRPF, and CISF.
  • Explaining the legal scope of AFSPA in conflict zones.
  • Handling a simulated local protest against military camps.
  • Detailing leadership strategies during counter-insurgency operations.

Reference Resources

  • Standard NCERT school textbooks (Class VI - XII)
  • Daily Newspapers: The Hindu or Indian Express
  • Official Government Portals (PIB, Yojana, Kurukshetra)
  • Mock test series papers and past year questions
  • Subject-specific standard reference books

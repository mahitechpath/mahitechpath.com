📋 Exam Pattern & Syllabus Overview

IBPS PO / SBI PO Exam. Prelims (English, Quant, Reasoning MCQ), Mains (Advanced Data Analysis, Reasoning, General Economy, and Descriptive Essay/Letter), and Group Discussion/Interview.

📖 Recommended Books List

  • • Quantitative Aptitude for Competitive Exams by Arun Sharma
  • • High School English Grammar by Wren & Martin
  • • Banking Awareness by Arihant Publications
  • • Online Quizzes for Daily Current Affairs & Financial News

🔑 Previous Year Paper Tips

Data Interpretation (DI) is the highest-weightage section in Mains. Master various charts (Radar, Caselet, Bar) and practice descriptive typing under a 30-minute timer.

🏆 Selection Process Steps

  • 1. Preliminary Written Exam (Speed MCQ Screening)
  • 2. Mains Written Exam (Objective Database Tests + Descriptive Writing)
  • 3. Group Exercise / Discussion (SBI PO specific)
  • 4. Personal Interview and Bank Preferences Allocation

🎯 Important Topics to Focus

Data Interpretation (DI) & Puzzle Solving, Syllogisms & Logical Deductions, Banking Terminology & RBI Regulations, Priority Sector Lending (PSL), Digital Banking & Cyber Security.

Exam Strategy & Selection Guide

Overview of the stages of selection, syllabus details, and recommended resources.

Syllabus Details

  • IBPS / SBI PO Phase 1 & 2

Exam Strategy & Selection Guide

Overview of the stages of selection, syllabus details, and recommended resources.

Exam Pattern & Syllabus

  • IBPS / SBI PO Phase 1 & 2
  • Advanced Data Interpretation (DI)
  • Logical Reasoning Puzzles
  • Banking Awareness & RBI Policies
  • Priority Sector Lending (PSL)
  • Descriptive English (Essay/Letter)

Core Selection Tips

  • Answering banking queries on repo rate hikes and deposit rates linkage.
  • Detailing KYC documents required to open a new current account.
  • Handling a simulated angry customer demanding fee reversals.
  • Comparing public sector banks and private sector banks operations.
  • Explaining priority sector lending targets for public banks.

Reference Resources

  • Standard NCERT school textbooks (Class VI - XII)
  • Daily Newspapers: The Hindu or Indian Express
  • Official Government Portals (PIB, Yojana, Kurukshetra)
  • Mock test series papers and past year questions
  • Subject-specific standard reference books

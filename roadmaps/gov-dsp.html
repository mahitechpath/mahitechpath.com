Exam Strategy & Selection Guide

Overview of the stages of selection, syllabus details, and recommended resources.

Exam Pattern & Syllabus

  • State PSC Group 1 Police
  • State Police Act & Manual
  • IPC & CrPC Local Applications
  • Crowd Control & Festival Security
  • Forensics and FSL Liaison
  • Local Intelligence Networking

Core Selection Tips

  • Justifying the use of force during a simulated violent protest.
  • Detailing the command structure of the state police department.
  • Explaining the process to register and investigate a cyber fraud FIR.
  • Discussing the causes of rising domestic violence and police responses.
  • Demonstrating physical standard benchmarks and leadership qualities.

Reference Resources

  • Standard NCERT school textbooks (Class VI - XII)
  • Daily Newspapers: The Hindu or Indian Express
  • Official Government Portals (PIB, Yojana, Kurukshetra)
  • Mock test series papers and past year questions
  • Subject-specific standard reference books

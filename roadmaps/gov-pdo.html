Exam Strategy & Selection Guide

Overview of the stages of selection, syllabus details, and recommended resources.

Exam Pattern & Syllabus

  • Panchayat Development Officer Core
  • 73rd Constitutional Amendment
  • State Panchayati Raj Act
  • MGNREGA & PMAY-G Schemes
  • Village GPDP Planning
  • Panchayat Accounting & Audit

Core Selection Tips

  • Explaining the process to select beneficiaries for rural housing schemes.
  • Detailing the sources of revenue for a Gram Panchayat.
  • Resolving a simulated dispute over drinking water connection priorities.
  • Comparing Gram Sabha and Gram Panchayat structures.
  • Demonstrating familiarity with local rural issues and crop seasons.

Reference Resources

  • Standard NCERT school textbooks (Class VI - XII)
  • Daily Newspapers: The Hindu or Indian Express
  • Official Government Portals (PIB, Yojana, Kurukshetra)
  • Mock test series papers and past year questions
  • Subject-specific standard reference books

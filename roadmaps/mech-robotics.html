Placement & Interview Strategy

Written tests, concept evaluations, and practical design challenges overview.

Resume Keywords

  • Robotic Linkage Design
  • DH Parameter Kinematics
  • Inverse Kinematics Solvers
  • Joint Torque Sizing
  • Servo & Stepper Controls
  • SolidWorks 3D Assemblies

Core Interview Focus

  • Assigning DH parameter frames to a given 3-axis robot arm diagram.
  • Explaining the physical meaning of Jacobian singularities.
  • Calculating required holding torque for a joint given link weights.
  • Contrasting hydraulic and electric actuators for heavy payloads.
  • Tuning PID controller gains to minimize joint tracking error.

Standard Preparation

  • Review textbooks and past exam question databases.
  • Practice timed numerical solving and multiple-choice questions.
  • Develop comprehensive subject-wise summary sheets.
  • Participate in mock tests to analyze error diagnostics.

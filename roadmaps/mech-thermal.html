Core Interview Focus

  • Deriving the thermal efficiency of an air-standard Diesel cycle.
  • Explaining the physical meaning of critical thickness of insulation.
  • Drawing velocity triangles for an impulse turbine stage and calculating work.
  • Comparing LMTD and NTU methods for sizing heat exchangers.
  • Detailing the physical significance of Prandtl and Grashof numbers.

Standard Preparation

  • Review textbooks and past exam question databases.
  • Practice timed numerical solving and multiple-choice questions.
  • Develop comprehensive subject-wise summary sheets.
  • Participate in mock tests to analyze error diagnostics.

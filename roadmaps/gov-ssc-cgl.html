📚 Exam Strategy & Preparation Guide

📋 Exam Pattern & Syllabus Overview

SSC CGL Tier 1 (Computer Based MCQ, 100 questions, Qualifying) and Tier 2 (Mathematical Abilities, Reasoning, English, General Awareness, and Computer Knowledge).

📖 Recommended Books List

  • • Quantitative Aptitude by R.S. Aggarwal
  • • Plinth to Paramount (English) by Neetu Singh
  • • Analytical Reasoning by M.K. Pandey
  • • Lucent's General Knowledge Reference Book
  • • Kiran Publication SSC Chapterwise Solved PYQs

🔑 Previous Year Paper Tips

Focus on speed and accuracy. Memorize formulas, shortcuts, and grammar rules. Solve 2 full mock exams weekly and analyze mistakes.

🏆 Selection Process Steps

  • 1. SSC CGL Tier 1 (Objective Computer-Based Test)
  • 2. SSC CGL Tier 2 (Main Written Test with Computer Proficiency)
  • 3. Physical/Medical standards check (for Inspector posts)
  • 4. Document Verification and Post Allocation

🎯 Important Topics to Focus

Percentage & Profit/Loss, Algebra & Trigonometry, English Grammar & Reading Comprehension, Coding-Decoding & Blood Relations, Indian Constitution & Science Basics.

Exam Strategy & Selection Guide

Overview of the stages of selection, syllabus details, and recommended resources.

Syllabus Details

  • SSC CGL Tier 1 & 2 Syllabus
  • Quantitative Aptitude & Algebra
  • English Grammar & Comprehension
  • Logical Reasoning & Deductions
  • General Awareness (Polity/History)
  • Computer Literacy (MS Excel/Word)

Core Selection Tips

  • Solving advanced quantitative questions under tight time limits.
  • Explaining the duties of an Income Tax Inspector or ASO in Secretariat.
  • Demonstrating quick logical deduction during pattern matching tests.
  • Explaining key constitutional amendments and current economic metrics.
  • Detailing methods to audit office expenditures and detect anomalies.

Reference Resources

  • Standard NCERT school textbooks (Class VI - XII)
  • Daily Newspapers: The Hindu or Indian Express
  • Official Government Portals (PIB, Yojana, Kurukshetra)
  • Mock test series papers and past year questions
  • Subject-specific standard reference books

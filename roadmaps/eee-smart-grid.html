Placement & Interview Strategy

Written tests, concept evaluations, and practical design challenges overview.

Resume Keywords

  • Smart Grid Technology
  • Advanced Metering Infrastructure
  • IEC 61850 Protocol
  • Distributed Energy Resources
  • Volt-Var Optimization
  • Substation Automation (SCADA)

Core Interview Focus

  • Explaining the AMI architecture and net metering data flow.
  • Describing the purpose of Phasor Measurement Units (PMUs).
  • Detailing the GOOSE messaging mechanism in IEC 61850.
  • Explaining the FLISR loop and how it restores power.
  • Discussing cybersecurity vulnerabilities in smart grid SCADA.

Standard Preparation

  • Review textbooks and past exam question databases.
  • Practice timed numerical solving and multiple-choice questions.
  • Develop comprehensive subject-wise summary sheets.
  • Participate in mock tests to analyze error diagnostics.
